Our Process

Our Process

Step 1

Choose Your Membership Pathway

Choose your membership plan.

Indulge in a curated mixer experience, bringing together VVS guests, celebrated designers, and top models.


Step 2

Submit Your Application

Applications can be submitted via email to join@vvslagos.com with subject line: “Partner Member Application ” or “Community Member Application ”

Step 3

Internal Review &Approval

All applications are reviewed by the VVS Lagos team. We assess:

Alignment with VVS values and cultural vision. Quality and originality of work

Strategic potential for partnership or community impact You will receive a confirmation within 7–10 working days.

Step 4

Welcome Package &Access

Upon approval, you will receive:

  • A digital welcome kit

  • Access to private VVS Lagos communications (Slack/Telegram or mailing list)

  • Invitations to upcoming mixers, talks, and creative labs

  • Opportunities to collaborate or co-create across VVS experiences

  • Priority access to event credentials, showcases, and publishing opportunities
