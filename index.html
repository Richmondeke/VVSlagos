The Sophomore Edition

Building on the success of its first edition in summer 2022, VVS Lagos returns to mesmerize fashion enthusiasts and cultural connoisseurs alike.

VVS, symbolizing rarity and authenticity, encapsulates the spirit of this unique event, nurturing communal growth within the rich and diverse African creative space.

Join us on July 7th & 8th to witness a grand spectacle featuring 10 Nigerian fashion houses, a conversation panel, a trunk sales event, an After Party and a VVS dinner.

Event Schedule

July 7th: Panel Conversations, VVS Lagos Fashion Night Out
July 8th: Art Exhibition, Trunk Sales, Runway Show, Honorary Awards, Music Show and After Party.
