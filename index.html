About VVS 2025

Building on the resounding success since it's inaugural edition in summer 2022 and its subsequent triumph in 2023 as well as the star studded 2024 edition, VVS Lagos is set to enchant fashion enthusiasts and cultural aficionados once again.

VVS, representing rarity and authenticity, epitomizes the essence of this exceptional gathering, fostering communal advancement within Africa's vibrant and diverse creative landscape.

Mark your calendars for July 7th as we invite you to a grand spectacle showcasing monumental art works, presentations from exclusive fashion houses, VVS Conversations, VVS Future labs, VVS Trunk show and culminating with the electrifying VVS After party with Spacestation
